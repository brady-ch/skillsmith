- Actions, undo, deferred work: load `command.md` or `memento.md`
- Notification/fan-out: load `observer.md` or `publish-subscribe.md`
- Policy/mode/workflow variation: load `strategy.md`, `state.md`, or `template-method.md`
- Grammar/traversal: load `interpreter.md`, `iterator.md`, or `visitor.md`
- Interaction/rules/specialists: load `mediator.md`, `specification.md`, `null-object.md`, or `blackboard.md`

## Stop Condition

1. one recommended pattern
2. one rejected nearby alternative
